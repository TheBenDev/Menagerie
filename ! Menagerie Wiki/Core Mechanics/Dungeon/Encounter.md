Encounters are nodes with random events that can be triggered throughout the run.
Some can only be seen on specific floors, but most can be encountered anywhere.




| Encounters          | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | Floor |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----- |
| Pool of murky water | Players can choose to spend 1 hour learning how to swim<br>If they swim for 10 hours they can learn waterwalking                                                                                                                      | Any   |
| Snitch!             | You encounter a noisy gnome. He wants to play a game of hide and seek. If you refuse, he'll alert the guards (Will have to fight guards)<br>If accepted he will be in a random non-empty node on the next floor. Find him for a prize | 1st   |
