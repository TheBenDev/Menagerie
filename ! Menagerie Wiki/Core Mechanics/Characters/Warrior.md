> A stance-based frontline that builds Rage to yield increased damage, protection, or control.

## Summary

Starter Ability:
Rage - Gain rage equal to 150% of damage taken and 50% of damage dealt. 
~~100 rage maximum~~ | 1 rage lost every second

Changing stances takes **5 seconds** and provides a unique buff

---
### Berserker

Berserker trades health and protection for enhanced damage and rage production, being able to launch flurries of attacks in rapid succession but at the risk of taking a lot of damage in the process.

Changing to Berserker stance gains 10 rage

Abilities:

Flurry: 5 seconds - Strike wildly for X damage (5% rage times rounded up)

### Duelist

Duelist trades constant damage or protection for control and tempo, being able to parry and counter attacks by landing at the same time as the enemies blows to control the battlefield, but struggles with large amounts of enemies.


Changing to Duelist stance makes your next action 50% faster rounded down

Abilities:

Pierce: 5 seconds - Deal 10 damage (ignores block)
