> A fragile damage specialist that builds Precision for enhanced burst, survival, and debuffs

## Summary

Starter Ability:
Precision - Gain a point of precision for every attack, increasing both Critical Strike chance and damage, scaling infinitely

Swapping weapons takes **5 seconds** and provides a unique buff

### Hunter

Hunter focuses on consistent pressure through repeated attacks, hitting multiple enemies, and setting traps that punish aggression.

Changing to Bow grants 5 Precision

Abilities:

Volley: 5 seconds - Shoots 3 arrows at one or more enemies

### Shadow

Shadow focuses on evasion, burst damage, and combo attacks. Using Precision to deal extra damage and exchange it for survival.

Changing to Daggers grants 5 Evasion for 10 seconds

Abilities:

Chain Attack: 5 seconds - Attack twice. If either hit crits, reduce the time of your next action.

### Toxic

Toxic focuses on applying poisons and debuffs to enemies to control the pace of the battle.

Changing to Darts reduces the speed of your next ability

Abilities:

Poison Dart: 5 seconds - Applies 5 poison to enemy and weakens the enemy.
