> A fragile but highly flexible spellcaster that uses Mana to control damage, tempo and resources through schools of magic.

## Summary

Starter Ability:
Mana - Spend mana to cast spells

Swapping schools takes **5 seconds** and provides a unique buff.

### Elemancy

Elemancy focuses on general-purpose elemental spellcasting, using Fire, Ice, and Earth magic to damage and debuff enemies.

Casting a spell switches to the next element, dealing damage with a different element will consume the previous passive to proc a secondary effect

- Fire: Applies burn and increases damage
- Ice: Slows targets action speed and refunds mana
- Earth: Applies Weaken and applies Vulnerable

Changing to Elemancy grants 10 Mana

Abilities: 

Elemental Bolt: 5 seconds, 10 MP - Deals X damage

### Chronomancy

Chronomancy focuses on manipulating the flow of time by controlling action speed, rewinding actions, and disrupting tempo


Changing to Chronomancy reduces the time cost of the next spell by X

Abilities:
